about me

I am a student at the University of Chicago Harris School of Public Policy, where I am currently studying for a Master of Science in Computational Analysis and Public Policy. I have a background in Middle Eastern art history, and I continue to use both my cultural studies background and my quantitative training in my work on the Cultural Amenities Project at the University of Chicago.

I am also particularly interested in the potential for data visualization and data journalism to create a more informed public. I created this site to display my ongoing data visualization work for the Chicago Policy Review and other data visualization and illustration projects.
